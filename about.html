About Us

Topopisy Sp. z o. o. is a young and dynamic company that specializes in selling pens with prints related to III LO in Gdańsk. Our main product is a pen decorated with a print and a logo, which is an ideal item for students, teachers, and fans of III LO in Gdańsk. We offer high-quality products and services to our clients and strive to provide the best customer experience possible. Our team consists of experienced specialists who are responsible for various areas of our business, such as customer contact, social media management, or contact with pen producers. As a company that does not have any material resources such as machines or raw materials, we rely on external suppliers or producers to be able to sell our products. Our business is not about profit, as we only aim to raise funds for a noble cause
